01 · About

I sit at the intersection of operations, data, and engineering, building tools that replace manual processes with reliable, automated ones.

I'm currently Junior Operations Manager and technical lead at SavvySaver, a South African rewards and cashback app, where I lead product testing, incident investigation, and reporting, and build the automation and AI tools the business runs on.

Before that, I worked as a Systems Reporting and Support Analyst at CSG, where I redesigned the timecard reporting process and cut missing or unapproved hours from roughly 13,000 to 2,000 in two months. I started my data career as a Data Science intern at ExploreAI Academy, leading ML project teams from data cleaning through to model deployment on AWS.

I hold a BSc in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Limpopo, and I'm AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst Associate (PL-300), and Microsoft Certified Power Platform Fundamentals (PL-900).
